Website V0

2020-01-11

Welcome to the site! I'm acquiring stuff about HTML and CSS to build a landing page. This is one of the victims. Look at that box-shadow and the @media queries for smaller screens. I've known HTML and CSS from a decade ago and I felt the need to refresh my knowledge. There's so much new stuff now and I'm really not the one to follow the news; I'm hoping to change that.
